Creators of This Project

This web platform was built as part of CodeSpark 2025, aiming to provide a smart, easy-to-use civic reporting system for urban communities. Citizens can report potholes, garbage, broken streetlights, and water leakages instantly, ensuring faster resolutions.

The creators of this web page are Pravallika Surabathula and Keerthana Veeravalli. Together, they envisioned a cleaner, safer, and smarter city.
